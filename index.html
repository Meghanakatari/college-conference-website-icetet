International Conference

The International Conference on Emerging Trends in Engineering & Technology (ICETET) will be held at Godavari Institute of Engineering and Technology(A), Rajahmundry (AP), India during Sep 10-11, 2024. The International Conference on Emerging Trends in Engineering & Technology (ICETET) is a prestigious gathering that serves as a platform for researchers, academicians, industry professionals, and students to present their latest findings, exchange ideas, and discuss the most recent innovations and developments in the field of engineering and technology. The primary objectives of ICETET are to promote collaboration among researchers and practitioners from around the world, foster interdisciplinary research, and facilitate the dissemination of knowledge and best practices in emerging areas of engineering and technology. Attendees can expect keynote sessions by renowned experts, paper presentations, poster sessions, workshops, and networking opportunities. By participating in ICETET, individuals can stay updated on the latest trends, challenges, and opportunities shaping the future of engineering and technology on a global scale.












About Giet (A)

Godavari Institute of Engineering and Technology(A), Rajahmundry (AP). GIET was established in the year 1998 to offer quality engineering education. It is located in the serene suburbs of Chaitanya Knowledge City, Rajahmundry on NH-16, the sprawling 300-acres of GIET campus. GIET is accredited by NAAC ‘A+’ Grade with CGPA 3.37 and NIRF ranked since 2019 consecutively. It is also recognized by the University Grants Commission 2(f) and 12(B) and is permanently affiliated to Jawaharlal Nehru Technological University, Kakinada (JNTUK). GIET has collaborations with many International organizations.
